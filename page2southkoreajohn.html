SOUTH KOREA

    Greeting

  • Greeting elders by waving is not considered polite.
  • Hugging is not a common way of greeting.

    • Table Manners

  • It is rude to refuse food or drinks at the dinner table.
  • When eating with a traditional Korean family, it is custom to wait until the eldest eats the first spoonful.

    • Giving and Receiving Gifts

  • The proper gift brought by Koreans to any family occasion is money.
  • Brand names are important in South Korea.
  • It is important to also wrap gifts.
